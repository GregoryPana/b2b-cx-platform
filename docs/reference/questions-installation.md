## Scoring Thresholds & Actions for Auditors

*- 4 to 5 (Pass - Excellent):* High-quality install. No further action needed.

*- 3 to 4  (Pass - Needs Improvement):* Minor aesthetic issues (e.g., a few loose clips) or customer education gaps. The QA auditor corrects minor issues on the spot and logs feedback for the Technician

*- 2  (Fail - Rework Required):* Significant physical or technical misses. A rework order is generated for the original contractor/ Staff to return and fix the installation.

*- 1  (Critical Fail):* Major safety violations, property damage, or severe network degradation. Triggers an immediate escalation, potential financial penalty or chargeback to the contractor, advisory or formal consequence management to the staff and requires urgent rework.
