# Governance Dashboard - Mystery Shopper Platform User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Mystery Shopper platform inside the Governance Dashboard.

---

## 1) Accessing Mystery Shopper Governance

1. Open the Governance Dashboard link.
2. If a security warning appears, click **Advanced**.
3. Click **Proceed to site** (or **Continue to website**).
4. Sign in with your work account.
5. Select **Mystery Shopper** from platform selection.

### Access Required

- Mystery Admin
- Super Admin

### Success Check

You should see these Mystery Shopper tabs at the top:

- Analytics
- Review Queue
- Locations and Purposes
- Survey Results

## 2) Quick Mystery Governance Workflow

2.1 Open **Analytics** to monitor service and location performance.

2.2 Open **Review Queue** to validate pending visits.

2.3 Open **Locations and Purposes** to maintain valid survey options.

2.4 Open **Survey Results** to audit finalized records.

Important: only approved surveys should influence reported performance.

---

## 3) Page: Analytics

3.1 Set date range.

3.2 Review service metrics and trend cards.

3.3 Check low-performing locations and waiting-time patterns.

### What you can do

- Track service quality over time.
- Identify weak locations and operational issues.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot approve surveys on this page.

### Images to place here

![Mystery Analytics](./images/user-guide/governance-mystery-02-analytics.png?v=20260420)

---

## 4) Page: Review Queue

4.1 Select a pending visit.

4.2 Review answers for consistency and quality.

4.3 Add review notes.

4.4 Select **Approve**, **Reject**, or **Needs Changes**.

### What you can do

- Validate survey quality before it reaches reporting.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot complete review without selecting a visit.

### Images to place here

![Mystery Review Queue](./images/user-guide/governance-mystery-03-review-queue.png?v=20260420)

---

## 5) Page: Locations and Purposes

5.1 Add or update locations.

5.2 Add or update visit purposes.

5.3 Activate or deactivate records.

### What you can do

- Maintain valid location and purpose options for surveys.

### What you cannot do

- Non-admin users cannot delete records.

### Images to place here

![Locations and Purposes](./images/user-guide/governance-mystery-04-locations-purposes.png?v=20260420)

---

## 6) Page: Survey Results

6.1 Filter by status and date.

6.2 Open result records.

6.3 Review response detail for quality checks.

### What you can do

- Audit historical records and completion quality.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot edit results directly from this page.

### Images to place here

![Mystery Survey Results](./images/user-guide/governance-mystery-05-survey-results.png?v=20260420)

---

## 7) Common Situations

- **No data shown:** Clear filters and reapply one at a time.
- **Missing visit:** Check status/date filters.
- **Action disabled:** Confirm role permissions.
- **Unexpected values:** Recheck date range and selected location scope.
