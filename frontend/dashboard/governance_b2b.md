# Governance Dashboard - B2B Platform User Guide

This guide explains how to use the B2B platform inside the Governance Dashboard.

---

## 1) Accessing B2B Governance

1. Open the Governance Dashboard link.
2. If a security warning appears, click **Advanced**.
3. Click **Proceed to site** (or **Continue to website**).
4. Sign in with your work account.
5. Select **B2B** from platform selection.

### Access Required

- B2B Admin
- Super Admin

### Success Check

You should see these B2B tabs at the top:

- Analytics
- Review Queue
- Businesses
- Visits
- Survey Results

## 2) Quick B2B Governance Workflow

2.1 Open **Analytics** to review trends and KPI performance.

2.2 Open **Review Queue** to validate pending submissions.

2.3 Open **Businesses** to maintain business master records.

2.4 Open **Visits** to plan and manage survey visits.

2.5 Open **Survey Results** to audit submitted records.

Important: only approved surveys are treated as official reporting data.

---

## 3) Page: Analytics

3.1 Set date range.

3.2 Review overview cards and KPI sections.

3.3 Filter by business.

3.4 Use question drilldown to inspect trends.

### What you can do

- Review performance trends and score movement.
- Analyze NPS, CSAT, relationship, and exposure patterns.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot approve surveys on this page.
- You cannot edit responses on this page.

### Images to place here

![B2B Analytics](./b2b-analytics.png?v=20260420)

---

## 4) Page: Review Queue

4.1 Open a pending visit.

4.2 Review responses and comments.

4.3 Add review notes.

4.4 Select **Approve**, **Reject**, or **Needs Changes**.

### What you can do

- Validate submission quality before approval.
- Control what data becomes official.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot complete review without selecting a visit.

### Images to place here

![B2B Review Queue](./b2b-review.png?v=20260420)

---

## 5) Page: Businesses

5.1 Create or edit business records.

5.2 Retire records no longer active.

5.3 Delete records only if you have admin permission.

### What you can do

- Maintain business data used by B2B surveys.

### What you cannot do

- Non-admin users cannot delete business records.

### Images to place here

![B2B Businesses](./b2b-business.png?v=20260420)

---

## 6) Page: Visits

6.1 Create a planned visit.

6.2 Assign representative and visit date.

6.3 Update or delete planned visits as needed.

### What you can do

- Plan and manage upcoming survey visits.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot change the business while editing an existing planned visit.

### Images to place here

![B2B Visits](./b2b-visits.png?v=20260420)

---

## 7) Page: Survey Results

7.1 Filter by status, business, and date.

7.2 Open a result record.

7.3 Review detailed responses and progress.

### What you can do

- Audit submitted records.
- Inspect full response details.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot edit results directly from this page.

### Images to place here

![B2B Survey Results](./b2b-results.png?v=20260420)

---

## 8) Common Situations

- **No data shown:** Clear filters and reapply one by one.
- **Numbers look wrong:** Check date range and selected business.
- **Record missing:** Verify status filter and date range.
- **Action disabled:** Confirm your role permissions.
