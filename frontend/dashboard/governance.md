# 📊 Governance Dashboard User Guide

Welcome to the Governance Dashboard.

This guide will help you:

- 📈 Review performance
- 🔍 Identify issues
- 🛠️ Assign actions
- ✅ Approve or reject survey results

No prior experience is required. Follow the steps in order for the best results.

---

## 🔐 1) Accessing the Dashboard

Follow these steps to open the dashboard:

1. Open the Governance Dashboard link shared by your manager.

2. If a security warning appears:
   - Click **Advanced**
   - Select **Proceed to site** (or **Continue to website**)

3. Sign in using your work account.

4. Wait for the dashboard to fully load.

✅ **Success check:** You should see the main dashboard with navigation options (Analytics, Actions, Review).

## 🔄 2) How the Dashboard Works (Simple Overview)

The Governance Dashboard follows a simple process:

1. 📊 **Review performance** (Analytics page)
2. ⚠️ **Identify problems** (low scores or declining trends)
3. 🛠️ **Create actions** (Actions page)
4. ✅ **Approve or reject surveys** (Review page)

⚠️ **Important rule:**  
Only **approved surveys** will appear in reports and analytics.

---

## 🧭 3) Step-by-Step Workflow

Use this workflow every time:

1. Select a **platform**:
   - B2B
   - Mystery Shopper
   - Installation Assessment

2. Go to the **Analytics page**

3. Set:
   - 📅 Date range
   - 🎯 Filters (if needed)

4. Review performance and identify weak areas

5. Create follow-up actions for those areas

6. Go to the **Review page**

7. Approve or reject surveys

8. Share actions with your team

---

## 📊 4) Page: Analytics

This is the starting point. It shows performance data and trends.

---

### 🎯 What this page is for

- Understanding overall performance
- Identifying weak areas
- Deciding where action is needed

---

### 🪜 How to use this page

1. Select the **platform** you want to review

2. Set the **date range**

3. Apply filters if needed:
   - Region
   - Location
   - Team

4. Review the **KPI cards** at the top  
   → These show overall performance

5. Review **trends and categories** below  
   → Look for:
   - Low scores
   - 📉 Downward trends
   - Repeated weak areas

👉 These are the areas that require action.

---

### ✅ What you can do

- Compare performance across platforms
- Focus on specific teams or regions
- Identify areas that need improvement

---

### ❌ What you cannot do

- Submit survey responses
- Edit survey data
- Approve or reject surveys

## 🛠️ 5) Page: Actions / Follow-Up

This is where you turn insights into actions.

---

### 🎯 What this page is for

- Turning insights into actions
- Assigning responsibility
- Tracking follow-ups

---

### 🪜 How to use this page

1. Select a **low-performing area**

2. Create an action:
   - ✏️ Write a short description of what needs to be done

3. Assign an **owner**  
   → The person responsible

4. Set a **due date**

5. Click **Save**

6. Confirm the action appears in the list

✅ **Success check:** The action is visible and assigned

---

### ✅ What you can do

- Create new actions
- Assign owners
- Set deadlines
- Track progress

---

### ❌ What you cannot do

- Save without required details
- Assign outside your permissions
- Complete work directly from this page

## ✅ 6) Page: Review (Approval Step)

This is the **most important step**.

It controls whether data appears in reports.

---

### 🎯 What this page is for

- Validating survey data
- Approving or rejecting surveys
- Finalizing what appears in analytics

---

### 🪜 How to use this page

1. Review the survey results carefully

2. Check:
   - Data accuracy
   - Completeness
   - Unusual values

3. Choose one option:
   - ✅ **Approve**  
     → Included in reports and analytics

   - ❌ **Reject**  
     → Not included  
     → May require correction

4. Confirm actions have been created (if needed)

5. Save your decision

---

### ✅ What you can do

- Approve valid surveys
- Reject incorrect surveys
- Control what appears in reports

---

### ❌ What you cannot do

- Edit survey responses
- Include data without approval
- Skip this step

---

⚠️ **Important:**

If a survey is **not approved**, it will:

- Not appear in dashboards
- Not affect KPIs
- Not be included in reports

## 🤝 7) Final Step: Team Handover

After completing your review:

1. Confirm all actions are:
   - Created
   - Assigned
   - Saved

2. Share the action summary with your team

3. Ensure owners understand their responsibilities

👉 The dashboard assigns actions — the team completes them.

---

## ⚠️ 8) Common Situations

### No data appears

- Clear filters
- Reapply one by one

---

### Numbers look incorrect

- Check platform
- Check date range

---

### Cannot assign an owner

- You may not have permission
- Contact admin if needed

---

### Permission error

- Take a screenshot
- Send to administrator

---

## 🆘 9) Getting Help

If you face any issues:

1. Take a screenshot

2. Note:
   - Page
   - Action performed

3. Send details to your administrator

---

## 🧠 10) Key Takeaways

- Start with **Analytics** 📊
- Create **Actions** 🛠️
- Finalize in **Review** ✅
- Only **approved surveys** affect reports

👉 Your role is to **identify issues and assign actions**, not fix everything inside the dashboard.
