# Mystery Shopper Survey User Guide

This guide explains the Mystery Shopper Survey in simple, non-technical steps.

---

## 1) Getting Access

1.1 Open the Mystery Shopper Survey link.

1.2 If a security warning appears, click **Advanced**.

1.3 Click **Proceed to site** (or **Continue to website**).

1.4 Sign in with your work account.

1.5 Confirm the Mystery Shopper page has loaded.

## 2) Quick End-to-End Flow

2.1 Start a new survey.

2.2 Complete visit details.

2.3 Score categories based on real observations.

2.4 Add comments for key findings.

2.5 Review progress.

2.6 Save Draft (if available) or Submit.

---

## 3) Page: Visit Details

3.1 Enter required details (location/date/basic visit information).

3.2 Confirm details are correct.

3.3 Continue to categories.

### What you can do

- Enter and verify visit details.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot submit the final survey from this page alone.

## 4) Page: Category Scoring

4.1 Open first category tab.

4.2 Score each question based on what was observed.

4.3 Add comments where needed.

4.4 Continue through all categories.

### What you can do

- Score service quality by category.
- Add comments to explain scores.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot use guessed answers for items not observed.
- You cannot leave required questions blank and submit.

## 5) Page: Review and Save/Submit

5.1 Check completion indicator.

5.2 Complete all required unanswered items.

5.3 Save Draft (if available) if continuing later.

5.4 Submit final response when complete.

### What you can do

- Save progress.
- Submit completed survey.

### What you cannot do

- You cannot edit finalized/locked submissions.

## 6) Common Situations

- **Situation:** A category was skipped.
  - **What to do:** Go back and complete required questions.

- **Situation:** You are unsure about a score.
  - **What to do:** Use on-screen scoring guidance and choose closest match.

- **Situation:** Work gets interrupted.
  - **What to do:** Save Draft before leaving.
